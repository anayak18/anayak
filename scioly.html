Science Olympiad: 2018 - present

Apurva Nayak July 24, 2021

I am a member of the WWP High School South Science Olympiad team. Science Olympiad is a competition that involves studying a particular field of science, and then testing one's knowledge of the subject at a competition.

I've competed and medalled in invitational, regional, and state competitions.

     Competed but did not place in Golden Gate and UPenn invitationals
     No regional tournament, understudy for states/national tournament

What I Learned

Despite its name, Science Olympiad has taught me more than just science. Of course, studying the same concepts I would learn in my school classes a year or two early has made me a better student, but I took away much more than just those science concepts. I learned how to work as a team, since all SciOly events run in either partnerships or groups of three. I came to learn how to communicate, delegate, and organize information. I also developed a better work ethic because the fast paced nature of the club required me to quickly familiarize myself with an event before competitions.
